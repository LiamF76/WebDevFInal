About Us

Welcome to Daraan Games, the premier source for all your D&D supplementary needs! Founded by an unlikely team of three raccoons in a trench coat, we bring you the most creative, outlandish, and meticulously crafted resources for your tabletop adventures.

The brains (and paws) behind the operation, Rufus, Patches, and Sprocket, started this venture in the back of a local tavern after realizing their shared love of storytelling and shiny objects. With an eye for detail and a knack for chaos, the trio has been crafting unique monsters, wild adventures, and magical items ever since.

At Daraan Games, we believe that every game should be as unpredictable and exciting as our own antics. Whether you're a seasoned Dungeon Master or a brand-new adventurer, our resources are here to help make your campaigns unforgettable.

Thank you for supporting our small but mighty team (seriously, we’re all under 3 feet tall). Remember, the only thing more powerful than a dragon is a raccoon with a vision—and maybe a Bag of Holding full of snacks.
